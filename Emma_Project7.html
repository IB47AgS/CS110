Analysis

Based on the boxplots, students who completed the test preparation course got higher scores in both reading and writing on average. We can clearly see the difference when we look at the boxplot ranges.

This evidences that taking the test prep course has a great impact on students' performance in language-related subjects.

However, this dataset does not tell us how long or how intense the preparation was. If we had more information, it could help us understand this pattern much better.
